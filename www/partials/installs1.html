• Create a Github account

    Github is the primary site that software developers throughout the world use to store their code, and share it with other developers. Visit the sign up page and create your own, free account.

  • Install Slack and join NSS channel

    1. Visit Slack and enter in your email address and "Nashville Software School" into the fields of the sign up form, and submit.

    2. On the sign in page, enter in nashvillesoftware into the field labeled teamdomain, and click Continue.

  • Create Treehouse account

    Visit treehouse and create an account with the Basic Plan, which is $25 per month.

  • Create Codepen account

    Visit the Codepen sign up page, and scroll all the way to the bottom to sign up for a free plan. If you like, you can click the "Use info from Github" button when creating your account.
